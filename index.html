4 Easy Steps to Make Tofu Spring Rolls

by N.D.

Posted on October 11, 2020 at 7:00 PM

First Step

See Below: (Ingredients)

  • 1 1/2 cups julienned vegetables (such as carrots, red pepper, and cucumber)
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 1 bunch fresh mint
  • 4 ounces vermicelli or rice noodles (the thinner the better)
  • 8-10 whole spring roll rice papers

Second Step

  • Chop all your vegetables
  • Boil 1 pot of water
  • Put rice noodles in water
  • Boil and stir frequently for 8-10 min

Third Step

  • Make your peanut sauce with peanut butter in pan
  • Add peanuts if you would like
  • Add some salt and pepper for flavor
  • Add red peppers to make it spicy *option*

Fourth Step

  • Boil hot water and then pour into a bowl
  • All your vegetables are chopped by now by your liking
  • Pour rice noodles into strainer and rinse with cold water
  • Take rice paper and dip it in the hot water that you boiled
  • Place wet rice paper on plate
  • Add chopped vegetables on your rice paper
  • Add tofu (wheter cooked or not) on it
  • Add lettuce and other toppings you would like
  • Then wrap up the rice paper like a burrito
  • Dip spring roll into peanut sauce
  • Enjoy!
